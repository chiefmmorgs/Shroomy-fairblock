ğŸ’¡ Pro Sticker Tips

  • Layer for depth: Put larger stickers in back, smaller ones in front
  • Use opacity: Make stickers semi-transparent for watermark effects
  • Match themes: Stick to related emoji for cohesive designs
  • Balance your layout: Use center guides to align elements
  • Size variation: Mix large and small stickers for visual interest
  • Color coordination: Use the Shroomy palette colors as inspiration
  • Rotation adds fun: Rotate stickers slightly for a playful look
