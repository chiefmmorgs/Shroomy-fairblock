üí° Pro Sticker Tips

  • Layer for depth: Put larger stickers in back, smaller ones in front
  • Use opacity: Make stickers semi-transparent for watermark effects
  • Match themes: Stick to related emoji for cohesive designs
  • Balance your layout: Use center guides to align elements
  • Size variation: Mix large and small stickers for visual interest
  • Color coordination: Use the Shroomy palette colors as inspiration
  • Rotation adds fun: Rotate stickers slightly for a playful look
  • Custom uploads: Convert the character designs to PNG stickers!

üé® Creating Custom Stickers

Want to turn the Shroomy character or your own art into stickers? Here's how:
  1. Start with a PNG: Make sure your image has a transparent background
  2. Optimal size: 500√ó500px to 1000√ó1000px works great
  3. Keep it simple: Clear shapes work better as stickers
  4. Remove backgrounds: Use a tool like remove.bg or Photoshop
  5. Save as PNG: Make sure to preserve transparency
  6. Upload: Click "Upload Custom Sticker" in the editor
